Across the world, children with language and speech communication needs tend to benefit from mainstream education with additional support mechanisms such as Text-to-Speech and Picture-to-Text application programs.
     
There are different types of learning disabilities but using a Text-to-Speech and Picture-to-Text application program; such children will be helped in improving the language as well as their ability to communicate effectively.
     
Our mission is to be able to make sure every child has easy access to these benefits and at the same time make it a platform for everyone else to be aware of different kinds of learning disabilities. And make this a platform for any problems or help that is needed.
